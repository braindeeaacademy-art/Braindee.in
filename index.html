Frequently Asked Questions

Why join BrainDEE?
Transform math from stress to power through neuro-cognitive training and ancient systems.
What is the ideal age for Abacus?
The ideal age is 5 to 13 years, as this is the period of peak neuro-plasticity and brain growth.
What is the use of Abacus in modern times?
It acts as a 'mental gymnasium' to develop focus, visualization, and photographic memory.
How does Vedic Maths help with school exams?
It provides shortcuts to solve complex arithmetic in seconds, saving time for tricky questions.
How often are classes held?
Classes are typically held twice a week for 2 hours each session, ensuring consistent practice.
Do you provide certificates?
Yes, BrainDEE Academy provides level-completion certificates after rigorous assessment tests.
Will it interfere with school syllabus?
No, it complements school math by making fundamentals stronger and calculations faster.
Can my child take both Abacus and Vedic Maths?
Yes, though we usually recommend Abacus for younger kids and Vedic Maths for 10+ years.
Is there homework for students?
Short daily 10-15 minute practice sessions are recommended to maintain speed and focus.
How long does each level take?
Each level typically spans 3-4 months depending on the child's learning pace and practice.
