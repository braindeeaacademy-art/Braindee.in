Gayatri

Founder & Director

My Journey & Vision

I’m Gayatri, a Pharmacy graduate, educator, entrepreneur, and wellness coach with over 15 years of teaching experience. Though my education is in pharmacy, my true passion has always been teaching and nurturing young minds.

Over the years, I have completed certifications in Montessori, NTT, child psychology, Abacus, and Vedic Maths to better understand how children learn and grow. Eight years ago, I started my journey in Abacus and Vedic Maths with a mission to help children overcome maths phobia, something I personally struggled with as a student.

Today, I proudly run BrainDEE, where I work passionately with multiple schools and students to support brain development, improve concentration, and make learning maths enjoyable and confidence-building.

Alongside education, I also work in the field of health and wellness, helping people transform their lifestyles and health naturally.

The Vision

"My vision is to contribute meaningfully to both the education system and people’s well-being by building confident students, healthier families, and a stronger future generation."
